Here's a time line of El Hom's life:

  • 1994 - Born in Convencion, Norte de Santander.
  • 2002 - Begins his studies at Guillermo Quintero Calderon school in his home town.
  • 2012 - Leaves his family's town to attend the UFPSO, thanks to his motivation to get ahead in life. Furthermore, that same he meets the friend who will accompany him for the rest of his life (El Hom).
  • 2016 - Graduates as an Environmental Engineer "alone" before anyone else, because he is stubborn and wanted to show off.
  • 2017 - Begins his first unemployment year alone because of his stubborness while his classmates continued studying.
  • 2018 - Moves to Aguachica - Cesar with his beloved woman "La Maye" and finds a job as a Chemestry teacher in a private school.
  • 2019 - His Brother from Another Mother (HOM) starts working as English Teacher for a colombian institution called SENA and he realizes that it is possible for him to apply too and improve his quality of life. So, he begins learning English as well.
  • 2020 - Quits his job at the school and manages to enter the SENA as an English teacher and becomes what at that moment he and his friend called a "Magnate".
